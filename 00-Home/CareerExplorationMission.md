# Career Exploration Mission

## Purpose

The purpose of this exploration is to determine whether robotics, embedded systems, intelligent machines, or systems engineering are strong career directions for me.

## Main Question

Do I enjoy building intelligent machines, designing complex systems, or both?

## Why I Am Interested

I am interested in engineering because I like building systems that combine software, hardware, automation, and intelligence. My projects such as Kairo, Orion, and my robotic arm suggest that I enjoy making software interact with the physical world.

## Career Paths I Am Testing

- Embedded Software Engineer
- Robotics Software Engineer
- Autonomous Systems Engineer
- AI Systems Engineer
- Robotics Systems Engineer
- Integration/Test Engineer

## What Success Looks Like

After six months, I should have:

- A clearer understanding of whether I enjoy robotics and embedded systems.
- At least one meaningful project prototype.
- A collection of notes, design decisions, and lessons learned.
- A better sense of what career roles fit me.
