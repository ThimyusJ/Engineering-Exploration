# Study Plan

## Mission

Build a small intelligent robot platform while learning the electronics, embedded software, robotics, and systems engineering concepts needed to design it properly.

## Time Split

- 50% building and testing
- 30% studying technical concepts
- 20% documenting systems engineering work

## Monthly Focus

| Month | Theme | Main Deliverable |
|---|---|---|
| Week 1 | Setup | Repository, templates, baseline |
| Month 1 | Electronics and motors | Motor Tester v1 |
| Month 2 | Embedded architecture | Structured firmware and test plan |
| Month 3 | Robot movement | Orion movement, status, obstacle basics |
| Month 4 | Systems engineering | Orion and Kairo architecture docs |
| Month 5 | Autonomy | One autonomous behavior |
| Month 6 | Capstone | Orion Rover v2 with documentation |

## Weekly Routine

- Study one or two focused topics.
- Build or test something small.
- Record questions and lessons learned.
- Update weekly review.
